selected project below

portrait of lost stars

2025

editorial & print design

‘Portrait of Lost Stars’ explores light pollution through photography, infographics and visual storytelling, presenting the loss of the night sky as both an environmental and cultural issue. Set in northern Italy, the project combines a narrative publication with a practical constellation guide to reconnect people with the night sky.

semester

2024

editorial design & installation

An editorial and spatial project developed for Milan Design Week, connecting emerging Swiss design with the international creative scene through print, digital publishing and installation.

castellinaria

2024

poster design & print

Created for the 37th edition of the Castellinaria youth film festival in Bellinzona, the project explores typography as the main visual element. Distorted and expanded letterforms generate rhythm, movement and visual tension, creating a bold and recognisable identity applied across both physical and digital festival spaces.

kizuna

2024

visual identity, artificial intelligence & web design

Kizuna blends Japanese tradition with a futuristic and urban aesthetic, transforming cultural symbols into contemporary visual codes. Developed through artificial intelligence and machine learning, the brand creates an evolving visual language that connects memory, technology and future visions.

ape theory

2025

photography

APE Theory explores the relationship between progress and primal instincts, reflecting on the thin line between evolution and regression. The project investigates the fragility hidden behind modern humanity, revealing the tensions and anxieties that emerge within an unstable contemporary world.

bodyline

2022

photography & form

Bodyline explores the natural lines and geometry of the human body through compositions shaped by posture, light and spatial balance. The project transforms curves, lights and forms into a dynamic visual language, creating expressive and harmonious images.
